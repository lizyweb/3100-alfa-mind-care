Frequently Asked Questions

  • WHAT TYPES OF SERVICES ARE OFFERED?

    Alfa Mind Care provides a comprehensive range of services, including brain profiling, physical wellness assessments, prenatal women’s health care, mental wellness support, addiction treatment, counseling, various therapies, and aftercare with follow-up services. Each service is tailored to address your specific needs and promote overall well-being.

  • HOW DO I SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT?

    To schedule an appointment, you can use our online booking system or contact our support team directly. Simply select the service you need and choose a convenient time slot. Our team will confirm your appointment and provide any additional information required to prepare for your session.

  • WHAT CAN I EXPECT DURING MY FIRST SESSION?

    During your first session, you will undergo an initial assessment to understand your needs and recoveries. This may include discussing your background, current challenges, and expectations from therapy. Our goal is to create a personalized plan that addresses your specific concerns and sets the foundation for effective support and progress.

  • HOW IS MY PRIVACY PROTECTED?

    At Alfa Mind Care, we prioritize your privacy and confidentiality. We employ advanced technology and stringent protocols to safeguard your personal information. To ensure your data is secure, we maintain secure servers and follow industry best practices. If you wish to release any information to a third party, please inform your therapist. They will provide you with an authorization form that must be completed and signed before any information can be shared. For more details on our privacy practices, please visit our SERVICES page.

  • HOW WILL I COMMUNICATE WITH MY THERAPIST?

    You can communicate with your therapist through several methods, depending on your preference:

    1, Messaging
    2, Live chat
    3, Phone calls
    4, Video conferencing

    These options are designed to provide flexibility and convenience in accessing your therapy sessions.
